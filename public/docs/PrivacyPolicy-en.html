Digital Life Project Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the "Digital Life Plan" website and related services. The purpose is to clearly explain to you how we will collect, use, store, share, transfer and protect your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to maintaining the security of your personal information. We hope that by reading this policy, you can fully understand the use of your information.

I. Personal information we collect and use

We will only collect and use your following personal information when necessary:

  1. Basic identity information

    2. When you create an account and use our products and services, we will collect your name, email, mobile phone number and other basic identity information to establish and verify your account identity so that you can log in normally and use various functions we provide. Serve.

  2. Personal attribute information

    3. In order to generate and continuously optimize your digital avatar, we will also collect personal attribute information such as avatar photos, voice samples, and personal profiles that you upload, and simulate your appearance characteristics and voice style by training model algorithms.

  3. User-generated content

    4. When you upload your own documents, product data and other content to our knowledge base, we need to collect this information to train digital avatars or virtual characters to provide you with more intelligent services during conversations.

  4. Social interaction information

    5. When you use our social features, we will generate and collect user interaction data such as your social dynamic content, likes and comment records.

  5. Other virtual character information

    6. If you also choose to create and set up additional virtual characters, we also need to collect the name, appearance, voice and other information of the character you set for character modeling so that it can talk according to the personalized configuration you specify.

  6. Device information

    7. When you visit our website or use related services, we will collect the device model, operating system version, unique device identifier, IP address, and device information used to calibrate time zone and language.

  7. Cookies

    8. In order to ensure the normal operation of the service functions, we will set necessary cookies on your browser or device to identify your account. You can choose to refuse or delete cookies in your browser settings.

  8. Location information

    9. After obtaining your explicit consent, we will also collect necessary geographical location information through GPS, Wi-Fi and other methods to implement specific functional services, such as providing you with virtual characters or interactive services in the surrounding environment.

  9. Usage logs

    10. In order to continuously optimize and improve your experience, we will collect necessary usage logs during your use of products and services so that we can analyze product problems and make improvements. These logs will not be associated with your identity.

    The above information will be collected and used strictly within the scope necessary to provide services. We will not collect any user information that is not relevant to the functionality of our products.

II. How we use your personal information

We will only use your personal information for reasonable, legitimate, legal and fair purposes such as providing, improving services, and enhancing user experience.

Specifically, we may use the information we collect to:

  1. Create and train your digital clone so that it can simulate your voice and appearance;
  2. Store and optimize the content you upload to the knowledge base so that the digital avatar can use this information to provide you with intelligent services;
  3. Display your social homepage and interactive information on the web page, such as your likes, comment records, etc.;
  4. Continue to improve our AI algorithms to enhance the conversation and reply capabilities of digital clones and virtual characters;
  5. Provide you with more personalized product recommendations or preferential information;
  6. Conduct necessary internal data analysis so that we can improve product design and user experience;
  7. Confirm your identity and ensure the security of your account;
  8. Realize the core functions of our products and maintain the normal operation of the products;
  9. Contact you and respond to your inquiries about our products or customer service requests;
  10. Determine and prevent any behavior that may be illegal or violate the agreement with us or our users;
  11. Comply with legal and regulatory requirements and perform relevant obligations.

We will not use the personal information collected for any other purpose beyond the above scope, nor will it violate the agreement with you.

IV. How we store and protect your personal information

  1. Information storage location

    2. We will only store your personal information within the territory of the People's Republic of China and will not transfer the data abroad.

  2. Information storage period

    3. We will only retain your personal information for the minimum period necessary to provide our services and comply with relevant laws. After this period, we will delete or anonymize the information that is no longer needed.

  3. Information security measures

    4. We attach great importance to the security of user information and take all reasonable and feasible security measures to prevent data from being leaked, damaged, lost, tampered with, misused and other risks. Specific measures include but are not limited to:

    1. Data encryption. We will use industry standard encryption technology to encrypt, save and transmit key information, including SSL, TLS, AES, etc.;
    2. Access control. We will establish a strict information access control mechanism and implement different authorization and approval systems for different levels of data;
    3. Data isolation. We will isolate, save and access the data of different users on the server;
    4. Data backup. We will establish an automated data backup mechanism to prevent information loss;
    5. Safety protection procedures. We will use various security protection technologies such as firewalls, intrusion detection, etc. to ensure information security;
    6. Data security audit. We will conduct regular security assessments and audits to continuously check and evaluate the information security status;
    7. Emergency response to security incidents. We have developed a complete emergency response plan for information security incidents to control the impact of security risks on users.

We will take remedial measures as soon as possible to reduce the impact of security incidents on you.
